How to Play YouTube Roulette

Welcome to YouTube Roulette! A fun and engaging game where you and your friends watch random YouTube videos and compete to select the best one. Here's how you play:

  • Joining a Game: Start a new game or join an existing one using a unique game code. Decide if you're playing locally (in the same room) or remotely.
  • Gameplay: Players take turns. When it's your turn, a random 4-character search term is generated. Use this term to find videos on YouTube.
  • Choosing Videos: You'll get three standard video options and up to three wildcards (like songs or playlists). Choose one to play for the group.
  • Watching Videos: Watch the selected video together. Any player can vote to end the video early, but it takes a majority vote to skip it before one minute has passed.
  • Superpowers: Each player has three superpowers to use once per game - Reroll, Replace, and Swap - allowing you to alter the search term to your advantage.
  • Scoring: After each round, players vote on their favorite video. The video with the most votes earns its selector one point. A unanimous vote earns two points.
  • Winning the Game: The first player to reach three points wins. Celebrate your victory, start a new game, or exit.

Get ready for unexpected fun and hilarious moments with YouTube Roulette!
